Stephen Michael Parkinson

Head trainer and founder of Howlin fitness and nutrition

Stephen was born in the UK and moved to china in his 30s. Having finished university and studying dance and drama, Stephen found he had a passion for health and nutrition.

He started Howlin Fitness, as a project during the globel pandemic of Covid19 as a way of motivating himself and other to stay fit and health while at home. This quickly lead to build a working enviroment for both himself and his wife,

Over the last few years the fitness company has grown from strength to strength moving from personal training in to gym wear and nutritional suppliments.
